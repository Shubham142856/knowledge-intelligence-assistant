VYOR ENTERPRISE HR & IT POLICY MANUAL (2026)

Section 1: Leave & Vacation Policy
- Employees are entitled to a maximum of 15 medical leaves per calendar year. Team Lead approval is mandatory for any leave exceeding 2 consecutive days.
- Annual paid vacation allowance is 20 days per year, accruing at 1.66 days per month worked.
- Maternity leave is provided for up to 26 weeks of fully paid leave. Paternity leave is provided for 4 weeks.

Section 2: Remote Work & Overtime Policy
- Remote work contracts have a maximum duration of 12 consecutive months before requiring formal renewal by the HR Department.
- Overtime work for senior engineers must be pre-approved by the Vice President of Engineering or the Engineering Operations Director. Standard overtime pay rate is 1.5x standard hourly wage.

Section 3: Data Security & System Access
- Customer database backups are encrypted at rest using AES-256-GCM encryption with keys managed in AWS KMS or GCP Cloud KMS.
- Remote access to production systems requires multi-factor authentication (MFA) via hardware security keys (FIDO2) and access through an encrypted Zero-Trust Network Access (ZTNA) tunnel.
- Customer records data retention policy mandates retaining data for 7 years post-contract termination, after which automatic GDPR-compliant permanent deletion occurs.

Section 4: Equipment & Intellectual Property
- Emergency hardware replacements in datacenters are handled 24/7 by the Datacenter Infrastructure Operations Team.
- All intellectual property, code, algorithms, and documentation developed by contractors or full-time employees during their engagement belong exclusively to VYOR AI.
- Standard onboarding duration for new engineering hires is 2 weeks, including mandatory security compliance training.

Section 5: Code of Conduct & Compliance
- Violating the code of conduct policy results in immediate disciplinary review, potential suspension, up to termination of employment without severance and legal escalation if applicable.
